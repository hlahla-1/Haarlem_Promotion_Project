Teylers Museum


Teylers Museum is an art, natural history, and science museum in Haarlem, Netherlands. Established in 1778, Teylers Museum was founded as a centre for contemporary art and science.

ABC Museum Centrum


The ABC Architectuurcentrum Haarlem is a center for Architecture in Haarlem, the Netherlands, dedicated to promoting interest and conserving the architectural heritage of Kennemerland.

Museum Haarlem


Museum Haarlem is the city museum of Haarlem and South Kennemerland. Here you can experience the history of Haarlem and reflect it on the present.
